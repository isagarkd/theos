Founder

Theos exists because most agencies split what should never be split: the strategy from the design, the design from the build, the build from the growth. Sagar founded the studio to carry one standard through all four — with AI woven into how the work gets made, not bolted on after.

The studio works as one integrated team across New Delhi and London. Small on purpose. Senior on purpose.
